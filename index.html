About Me

I am a dedicated cybersecurity professional with over 2.5 years of experience in identifying, analyzing, and mitigating security vulnerabilities across diverse digital environments. My expertise spans web and mobile application penetration testing, infrastructure and network security, and security operations center (SOC) management.

Passionate about safeguarding digital assets, I have worked on projects in various industries, including finance, insurance, manufacturing, cryptocurrency, and social media. My approach emphasizes proactive threat detection, effective vulnerability reporting, and the implementation of robust security measures to ensure comprehensive protection for organizations.

Driven by a commitment to continuous learning, I am always exploring the latest advancements in cybersecurity to stay ahead of emerging threats and enhance my skills in this dynamic field.
